# Revenue Optimization Plan for PlanYourPark.com

## Current Revenue Streams

### 1. Undercover Tourist Affiliate (CJ Network)
- **Commission:** ~5-8% on ticket sales
- **Cookie Duration:** 45 days
- **Average Order Value:** $400-800 (family tickets)
- **Current Status:** Active on most pages

### 2. Google AdSense
- **Status:** Code installed, limited placement
- **Potential:** $2-5 RPM (low for this niche)
- **Priority:** Secondary to affiliates

### 3. Email List
- **Status:** Formspree forms active
- **Growth:** Minimal (no lead magnet)
- **Monetization:** Not yet implemented

## Immediate Revenue Opportunities

### 1. Affiliate Link Expansion (Week 1)

#### Add Links to Existing Content
Every blog post should have:
- [ ] Inline CTA after intro paragraph
- [ ] Bottom CTA after content
- [ ] Contextual links within content (2-3 per post)

#### New Affiliate Programs to Join

| Program | Network | Commission | Fit |
|---------|---------|------------|-----|
| Undercover Tourist | CJ | 5-8% | Primary - already active |
| Get Away Today | CJ | 4-6% | Alternative ticket seller |
| Park Savers | Private | 5% | Another ticket option |
| Booking.com | Partner | 4% | Hotels |
| Hotels.com | CJ | 4-5% | Hotels |
| RentalCars.com | CJ | 5% | Car rentals |
| Amazon Associates | Amazon | 1-4% | Disney gear, packing items |

**In Blog Posts:**
- "Where to stay" → Hotels.com affiliate
- "How to get there" → RentalCars.com
- "What to pack" → Amazon (sunscreen, strollers, etc.)
- "Save money" → Undercover Tourist

### 2. Lead Magnet + Email Funnel (Week 2)

**Format:** PDF + email series
**Content:**
- Printable packing checklist
- 7-day itinerary template
- Height requirement chart

- Restaurant reservation tracker

#### Landing Page
- Dedicated page: `/disney-planning-toolkit`

- Preview of what's inside

#### Email Welcome Sequence

**Email 1 (Immediate):** Deliver toolkit
- Subject: "Your Disney Planning Toolkit is here!"
- Attach PDF
- Set expectations for series

**Email 2 (Day 2):** Best tips
- Subject: "The #1 mistake first-timers make"

**Email 3 (Day 4):** Money saving
- Subject: "How we saved $300 on our Disney trip"

- Direct affiliate link

**Email 4 (Day 7):** Character dining
- Subject: "Is character dining worth it?"

**Email 5 (Day 10):** Final tips
- Subject: "Last-minute Disney trip checklist"

### 4. Conversion Rate Optimization

#### A/B Test Ideas

**CTA Button Text:**
- "Get Discounted Tickets" vs "Save on Tickets" vs "Buy Tickets"

**CTA Placement:**
- After 2nd paragraph vs after 4th paragraph
- Sticky sidebar CTA vs inline only

**Email Capture:**
- Popup vs inline form
- Exit-intent vs time-delay
- Lead magnet vs "join newsletter"

#### Heatmap Tracking
Set up Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity to track:
- Where users click
- How far they scroll
- Where they drop off
