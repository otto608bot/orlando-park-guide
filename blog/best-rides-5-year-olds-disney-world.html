Magic Kingdom: Where the Magic Begins

Magic Kingdom is the heart of Disney World and the park where you'll find the most ride options perfect for 5-year-olds.

Peter Pan's Flight

Flying over London in a pirate ship and soaring through Neverland captures the imagination like few other rides can. Parent tip: The queue can stretch to 60+ minutes. Consider using Genie+ for this one.

The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh

This gentle dark ride takes you through the Hundred Acre Wood in a honey pot vehicle. Height requirement: None

Pirates of the Caribbean

Don't let the name fool youâ€”this boat ride is surprisingly mellow for 5-year-olds. Sit toward the middle or back of the boat to minimize splash from the drop.

Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin

Interactive rides are gold for 5-year-olds. Kids get competitive trying to rack up points shooting at targets.

Hollywood Studios: Action and Adventure

Toy Story Mania!

This 4D carnival-style game is an absolute must-do. The competition factor makes it fun for the whole family.

Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway

Disney's newest trackless ride is pure joy from start to finish. Height requirement: None

Animal Kingdom: Wild Encounters

Kilimanjaro Safaris

A 20-minute open-air truck tour through an African savanna with real animals. Best time: early morning or late afternoon.

Na'vi River Journey

A gentle boat ride through the bioluminescent forests of Pandora. Height requirement: None

EPCOT: World Discovery

Frozen Ever After

A boat ride through Arendelle featuring Anna, Elsa, and Olaf. Two small backward drops that are very mild.

Essential Tips for Parents

Height Requirements to Know:

  • 32 inches: Alien Swirling Saucers
  • 40 inches: Slinky Dog Dash, Seven Dwarfs Mine Train

Rider Switch: Use Rider Switch for rides your 5-year-old can't do. One parent waits while the other rides, then you swap without waiting again.
