Comfort Tips for Pregnant Moms

Stay Hydrated and Fueled

Florida heat and pregnancy are challenging. Bring a refillable water bottle and get free ice water at any quick-service restaurant. Pack pregnancy-friendly snacks.

Use Baby Care Centers

Magic Kingdom's Baby Care Center (between Casey's Corner and The Crystal Palace) offers private nursing rooms, changing stations, and quiet air-conditioned space to rest.
