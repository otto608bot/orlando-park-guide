Plan Your Park

Automation & Scaling Strategy

Prepared for Bennett, VP of Product Strategy

Core Thesis

Transforming manual content creation into engineered, scalable revenue machines.

  • Shift from labor-intensive processes to automated systems
  • Leverage AI for asymmetric revenue growth
  • Focus on high-ROI pipelines for sustainable scaling

Pipeline 1: Automated SEO Content Engine

Sub-agent drafting & PR generation

  • AI-driven blog post creation targeting high-traffic keywords
  • Automated PR submissions to boost domain authority
  • Goal: 50+ monthly posts with minimal human oversight

Pipeline 2: Affiliate Link Injection

Automating Amazon Associates monetization

  • Dynamic insertion of affiliate links in content
  • Contextual relevance to maximize click-through rates
  • Projected: 15% revenue lift per visitor via optimized placements

Pipeline 3: Programmatic SEO

Generating 100+ highly specific landing pages

  • Extract ride data from app.js to create targeted pages
  • Capture long-tail search traffic (e.g., "best rides for toddlers at Disney")
  • Scalable to thousands of niche queries with low competition

Pipeline 4: Operational Maintenance

Content freshness, price monitoring, rank tracking

  • Automated updates to keep content current (e.g., park hours, ticket prices)
  • Monitor affiliate link performance and SEO rankings
  • Ensure sustained traffic and revenue with zero manual effort

Next Steps & ROI

Which machine do we engineer first?

  • SEO Content Engine: Fastest to implement, immediate traffic impact
  • Affiliate Injection: Highest per-visitor revenue potential
  • Programmatic SEO: Long-term traffic domination
  • Operational Maintenance: Sustains all other pipelines
