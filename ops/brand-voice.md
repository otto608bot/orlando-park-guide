# Brand voice

## Positioning
Practical Orlando planning for **parents** — which park fits *your* kids and which rides they’ll enjoy.

## Voice
- Warm, clear, direct; “we” is fine  
- Specific over vague (“under 40 inches”, “one park day with a preschooler”)  
- No hype, no fake urgency  

## Family lore policy (important)
Referencing “our kids” is allowed **only** if we keep a stable, non-contradictory profile.

Until an explicit family profile is approved, **prefer**:
- “kids this age…”
- “if your 5-year-old…”
- “families in our situation often…”

Avoid inventing ages, names, or trip years that can conflict across posts.

## Visual style
Heroes: bright, family theme-park energy consistent with existing custom blog heroes (not generic stock sludge). Generate per-post; match lighting/color energy of recent packing / Universal posts.

## Claims
- Heights/policies: cite or date  
- Medical/pregnancy: cautious, not medical advice  
- Affiliates: disclosed
