      <p>Packing for Disney World isn't like packing for a typical vacation. Between the Florida heat, unpredictable rain, and 12-hour park days, you need to be strategic about what goes in your suitcase.</p>

      <p>After dozens of Disney trips with my own family—and learning the hard way what we actually needed—I've compiled the ultimate packing list. This covers everything from park bag essentials to things you probably haven't thought of.</p>

      <h2>The Disney World Packing Philosophy</h2>

      <p>Before we dive into the list, here's the mindset: <strong>pack light, but pack smart</strong>. You'll be walking 8-12 miles per day, carrying bags through security, and navigating crowded parks. Every item should earn its place.</p>

      <div class="parent-tip">
        <strong>Parent tip:</strong> We keep a "park bag" that stays packed with essentials (<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B005IHT94S?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sunscreen</a>, <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CZ9LV3H2?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">portable charger</a>, snacks) so we can grab it and go each morning.       </div>

      <h2>What NOT to Pack</h2>

      <p>Just as important as what to bring—here's what to leave home:</p>

      <ul>
        <li><strong>New shoes</strong> – Break them in first or you'll regret it</li>
        <li><strong>Heavy jackets</strong> – Even in winter, Florida is mild during the day</li>
        <li><strong>Valuable jewelry</strong> – Easy to lose on rides</li>
        <li><strong>Large bags</strong> – You'll carry it all day; keep it minimal</li>
        <li><strong>Food for the whole trip</strong> – Grocery delivery to your resort is easier</li>
        <li><strong>Ponchos from home</strong> – Grab a <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B018THPISO?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">cheap 20-pack on Amazon</a> instead—way cheaper than buying at the parks</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Final Tips for Packing Success</h2>

      <ol>
        <li><strong>Roll, don't fold</strong> – Saves space and reduces wrinkles</li>
        <li><strong>Pack outfits, not items</strong> – Plan what you'll wear each day</li>
        <li><strong>Leave room for souvenirs</strong> – You'll buy more than you think</li>
        <li><strong>Ship items ahead</strong> – Order your <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B005IHT94S?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sunscreen</a>, <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B018THPISO?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ponchos</a>, and essentials on Amazon and deliver straight to your Disney resort</li>
        <li><strong>Pack a "first night" bag</strong> – Essentials in case luggage is delayed</li>
      </ol>

      <p>Remember: you can buy almost anything at Disney World (for a premium). Don't stress if you forget something—focus on the essentials and the memories you'll make.</p>
