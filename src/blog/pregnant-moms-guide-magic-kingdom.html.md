    <h2>Pregnancy-Safe Rides at Magic Kingdom</h2>
    <p><strong>The general rule:</strong> If a ride has no height requirement, it's typically safe for pregnant women. These gentle attractions are designed for guests of all ages.</p>

    <h3>Fantasyland Favorites</h3>
    
    <p><strong>"it's a small world"</strong> — This iconic boat ride is the definition of pregnancy-friendly. Glide through scenes representing cultures from around the world.</p>

    <p><strong>The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh</strong> — Hop into a honey pot and journey through the Hundred Acre Wood. Gentle movements and charming scenes.</p>

    <p><strong>Peter Pan's Flight</strong> — Soar over London and Neverland in your own pirate ship. The movement is slow and smooth with no sudden drops.</p>

    <p><strong>Under the Sea ~ Journey of The Little Mermaid</strong> — Clam-shell vehicles take you through Ariel's underwater world. Entirely smooth with no drops.</p>

    <h3>Tomorrowland Treasures</h3>

    <p><strong>Tomorrowland Transit Authority PeopleMover</strong> — This elevated tram ride is a pregnant mom's best friend. A 10-minute break off your feet with scenic views.</p>

    <p><strong>Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin</strong> — Help Buzz defeat Emperor Zurg. You control the spinning, so keep it minimal if needed.</p>

    <h2>Rides to Avoid While Pregnant</h2>

    <p>According to Disney's official guidelines, expectant mothers should <strong>not ride</strong>:</p>

    <ul>
      <li><strong>Space Mountain</strong> — High speeds, sharp turns, sudden drops</li>
      <li><strong>Big Thunder Mountain Railroad</strong> — Sharp turns and drops</li>
      <li><strong>Seven Dwarfs Mine Train</strong> — Swinging cars and drops</li>
      <li><strong>TRON Lightcycle / Run</strong> — High-speed launch coaster</li>
      <li><strong>Tiana's Bayou Adventure</strong> — Multiple drops including 50-foot plunge</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Comfort Tips for Pregnant Moms</h2>

    <h3>Stay Hydrated and Fueled</h3>
    <p>Florida heat and pregnancy are challenging. Bring a <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B006ZT8MKU?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">refillable water bottle</a> and get free ice water at any quick-service restaurant. A <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B001B5I57I?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">cooling towel</a> around your neck is a lifesaver in the Florida heat. Pack pregnancy-friendly snacks.</p>

    <h3>Use Baby Care Centers</h3>
    <p>Magic Kingdom's Baby Care Center (between Casey's Corner and The Crystal Palace) offers private nursing rooms, changing stations, and quiet air-conditioned space to rest.</p>
