    <h2>Magic Kingdom: Perfect for Little Ones</h2>
    <p>Magic Kingdom is the ultimate destination for toddlers. More than half the rides here are designed with young children in mind.</p>

    <h3>"it's a small world"</h3>
    <p>The quintessential toddler ride. Gentle boat cruise through rooms filled with singing dolls from around the world. The song will be stuck in your head for days, but the look of wonder on your toddler's face is worth it. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh</h3>
    <p>Hop into a honey pot and journey through the Hundred Acre Wood. Bright colors, familiar characters, and a gentle pace make this a toddler favorite. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Peter Pan's Flight</h3>
    <p>Soar over London in a pirate ship and through Neverland. The flying sensation captivates toddlers without being scary. <strong>Parent tip:</strong> Use Genie+ for this one—lines get long fast.</p>

    <h3>Dumbo the Flying Elephant</h3>
    <p>A classic for a reason. Toddlers love controlling how high their elephant flies. The indoor play area while you wait is a lifesaver for wiggly little ones. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Mad Tea Party</h3>
    <p>Spin in a giant teacup—you control the speed. Toddlers love the colors and the gentle spinning (just don't overdo it!). <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Under the Sea ~ Journey of The Little Mermaid</h3>
    <p>Clam-shell vehicles take you through Ariel's world with all the classic songs. Entirely smooth with no drops or surprises. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>Disney's Animal Kingdom: Wild and Gentle</h2>

    <h3>Na'vi River Journey</h3>
    <p>A peaceful boat ride through the bioluminescent forests of Pandora. The glowing plants and gentle music mesmerize toddlers. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Kilimanjaro Safaris</h3>
    <p>See real animals from an open-air vehicle. Toddlers love spotting giraffes, elephants, and lions. Can be bumpy—hold tight to little ones. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>TriceraTop Spin</h3>
    <p>Like Dumbo, but with dinosaurs. Toddlers control how high their triceratops flies. Usually short waits. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>EPCOT: World Discovery</h2>

    <h3>Frozen Ever After</h3>
    <p>A boat ride through Arendelle with Anna, Elsa, and Olaf. Two small backward drops that are very mild. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>The Seas with Nemo & Friends</h3>
    <p>Climb into a "clamobile" and search for Nemo through underwater scenes. Ends at a real aquarium—toddlers love watching the fish. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Gran Fiesta Tour Starring The Three Caballeros</h3>
    <p>A gentle boat ride through Mexico with Donald Duck. Usually no wait and a nice break from the heat. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>Hollywood Studios: Limited but Fun</h2>

    <h3>Toy Story Mania!</h3>
    <p>4D carnival-style shooting game. Toddlers love the colors and pulling the string—even if they don't understand the scoring. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway</h3>
    <p>Disney's newest trackless ride is pure joy. No scary moments, just Mickey and friends in a fun adventure. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>Essential Tips for Parents</h2>

    <h3>Rider Switch is Your Friend</h3>
    <p>If you have kids of different ages, use Rider Switch. One parent rides with the older kid while the other waits with the toddler, then you swap without waiting again.</p>

    <h3>Best Times for Toddlers</h3>
    <p>Early morning (rope drop) and late evening (after dinner) have the shortest lines. Toddlers do best when it's not packed and hot.</p>

    <h3>Don't Skip the Breaks</h3>
    <p>Toddlers need downtime. Plan for a midday break at your hotel, or find a quiet spot in the park. The Baby Care Centers are lifesavers.</p>

    <h3>Pack Smart</h3>
    <p>Bring snacks, a change of clothes, and their comfort item. A <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08866RDYK?tag=planyourpark-20" target="_blank" rel="noopener">clip-on stroller fan</a> is essential for keeping little ones cool in the Florida heat. Disney is magical but overstimulating—familiar items help.</p>

    <hr>

    <h2>Ready to Plan Your Trip?</h2>
    <p>Disney World with a toddler is exhausting but unforgettable. Focus on these gentle rides, take breaks, and don't try to do everything. The memories you'll make are worth every moment.</p>
