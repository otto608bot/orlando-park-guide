Questions About Ride Closures?

Ask below and we'll answer. Your question might help other visitors too!

Q: How do I know if a ride is open when I get to the park?
A: Download the official Disney World and Universal Orlando apps before your trip. They show real-time wait times and ride status. We also recommend checking the morning of your visit—refurbishments can sometimes end early or get extended without notice.
Q: Are there any rides that are rarely closed for refurbishment?
A: Popular headliners like Avatar Flight of Passage, Seven Dwarfs Mine Train, and Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey tend to have shorter maintenance windows and are rarely closed for extended periods. However, even these can close unexpectedly, so always check the app on your visit day.
