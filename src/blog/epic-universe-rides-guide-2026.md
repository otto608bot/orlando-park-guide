    <p>Universal Epic Universe is finally here, and it’s changing the game for Orlando vacations. With five immersive lands and over 50 unique experiences, it’s a lot to take in. As a parent who has spent years navigating the existing Universal parks, I know the biggest question on everyone's mind is: <strong>What are the rides actually like?</strong></p>

    <p>In this guide, we’re breaking down every confirmed ride at Epic Universe by land, including height requirements, thrill levels, and those essential "parent tips" that will save your sanity in the Florida heat.</p>

    <div class="parent-tip">
      <h4>💡 Pro Parent Tip</h4>
      <p>Epic Universe is huge. Unlike Universal Studios Florida, which is a relatively easy loop, Epic Universe uses a "hub and spoke" design. Plan to spend at least two full days here if you want to experience every ride without rushing.</p>
    </div>

    <h2>Celestial Park: The Gateway</h2>
    <p>Celestial Park is the heart of Epic Universe. It’s not just a walkway; it’s a lush, water-filled land with its own world-class attractions.</p>

    <h3>Starfall Racers</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Dual-launching Racing Coaster | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 48" (122cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> High
    </div>
    <p>This is the park's crown jewel coaster. Two tracks race side-by-side at speeds up to 62 mph, reaching heights of 133 feet. The "Celestial Spin" maneuver, where the two cars cross over each other while inverted, is a world-first and absolutely breathtaking.</p>
    <img src="/images/rides/eu-stardust.jpg" alt="Starfall Racers Coaster" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:1rem;">

    <h3>Constellation Carousel</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Carousel | <strong>Height Req:</strong> None | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Low
    </div>
    <p>This isn't your average carousel. The animals are replaced by celestial creatures that rotate 360 degrees as they glide, making it feel like you're floating through space. It’s the perfect first "ride" for toddlers.</p>

    <h2>Super Nintendo World</h2>
    <p>Step through the green pipe and into the most interactive land ever built. This is every Mario fan's dream come true.</p>

    <h3>Mario Kart: Bowser’s Challenge</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Augmented Reality Dark Ride | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 42" (107cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Medium
    </div>
    <p>Combining physical sets with AR goggles, you’ll race alongside Mario and Princess Peach to defeat Team Bowser. You’ll actually steer your kart and throw shells at targets.</p>
    <div class="ride-image-grid">
      <img src="/images/rides/eu-mario-kart.jpg" alt="Mario Kart Ride Entrance">
      <img src="/images/rides/eu-yoshi.jpg" alt="Yoshi's Adventure">
    </div>

    <h3>Yoshi’s Adventure</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Omnimover | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 36" (92cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Low
    </div>
    <p>A gentle trek through the Mushroom Kingdom. You’ll board a Yoshi and hunt for three glowing eggs. This offers the best views of the land for photos!</p>

    <h3>Mine Cart Madness (Donkey Kong Country)</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> "Jumping" Coaster | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 44" (112cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> High
    </div>
    <p>A revolutionary coaster design that makes it look like your cart is "jumping" over gaps in the track, just like in the Donkey Kong Country games. It’s fast, frantic, and fun.</p>
    <img src="/images/rides/eu-mine-cart.jpg" alt="Donkey Kong Mine Cart Coaster" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:1rem;">

    <h2>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter – Ministry of Magic</h2>
    <p>Set in 1920s Paris (from the Fantastic Beasts films) and the 1990s British Ministry of Magic, this is the most sophisticated Potter land yet.</p>

    <h3>Harry Potter and the Battle at the Ministry</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> High-Tech Dark Ride | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 40" (102cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Medium/High
    </div>
    <p>Traveling by Floo Network, you’ll enter the Ministry just as Dolores Umbridge is standing trial. When she attempts to escape, you join Harry, Ron, and Hermione in a chase through the Ministry’s many departments. The scale of the sets is jaw-dropping.</p>
    <img src="/images/rides/eu-battle-ministry.jpg" alt="Ministry of Magic Ride" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:1rem;">

    <h2>How to Train Your Dragon – Isle of Berk</h2>
    <p>The perfect land for families. It feels exactly like the movies, complete with life-sized dragons everywhere.</p>

    <h3>Hiccup’s Wing Gliders</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Launched Family Coaster | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 40" (102cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Medium
    </div>
    <p>Feel what it's like to fly a dragon. This coaster swoops over the lagoon and through the village. It’s thrilling but accessible for younger kids.</p>
    <img src="/images/rides/eu-hiccup.jpg" alt="Hiccup's Wing Gliders" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:1rem;">

    <h3>Fyre Drill</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Boat Ride / Water Battle | <strong>Height Req:</strong> None | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Low
    </div>
    <p>Board a Viking boat and use water cannons to blast targets (and other guests). You WILL get wet on this one!</p>
    <img src="/images/rides/eu-fyre-drill.jpg" alt="Fyre Drill Boat Ride" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin-bottom:1rem;">

    <h2>Dark Universe</h2>
    <p>For the fans of the classic Universal Monsters. This land is moody, foggy, and brilliantly creepy.</p>

    <h3>Monsters Unchained: The Wolf Man Experiment</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Thrill Dark Ride | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 48" (122cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> High
    </div>
    <p>Deep inside Frankenstein Manor, Dr. Victoria Frankenstein’s experiment has gone wrong, and all the monsters are loose. This is widely considered the most intense dark ride in the park. Be warned: it might be too scary for small children.</p>
    <div class="ride-image-grid">
      <img src="/images/rides/eu-monsters.jpg" alt="Monsters Unchained Entrance">
      <img src="/images/rides/eu-werewolf.jpg" alt="Curse of the Werewolf Coaster">
    </div>

    <h3>Curse of the Werewolf</h3>
    <div class="ride-meta">
      <strong>Type:</strong> Spinning Coaster | <strong>Height Req:</strong> 40" (102cm) | <strong>Thrill:</strong> Medium
    </div>
    <p>A family-friendly spinning coaster that takes you through the woods as you try to escape a pack of werewolves. The spinning isn't too intense, but it adds a great layer of fun.</p>

    <div class="parent-tip">
      <h4>🎟️ Ticket Strategy</h4>
      <p>Universal is currently requiring a multi-day ticket to access Epic Universe. You cannot buy a single-day ticket for this park yet. We recommend the 3-Day 3-Park ticket to see everything.</p>
