    <h2>Magic Kingdom: Where the Magic Begins</h2>
    <p>Magic Kingdom is the heart of Disney World and the park where you'll find the most ride options perfect for 5-year-olds.</p>

    <h3>Peter Pan's Flight</h3>
    <p>Flying over London in a pirate ship and soaring through Neverland captures the imagination like few other rides can. <strong>Parent tip:</strong> The queue can stretch to 60+ minutes. Consider using <a href="https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-genie-plus/">Lightning Lane</a> for this one.</p>

    <h3>The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh</h3>
    <p>This gentle dark ride takes you through the Hundred Acre Wood in a honey pot vehicle. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h3>Pirates of the Caribbean</h3>
    <p>Don't let the name fool you—this boat ride is surprisingly mellow for 5-year-olds. Sit toward the middle or back of the boat to minimize splash from the drop.</p>

    <h3>Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin</h3>
    <p>Interactive rides are gold for 5-year-olds. Kids get competitive trying to rack up points shooting at targets.</p>

    <h2>Hollywood Studios: Action and Adventure</h2>
    
    <h3>Toy Story Mania!</h3>
    <p>This 4D carnival-style game is an absolute must-do. The competition factor makes it fun for the whole family.</p>

    <h3>Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway</h3>
    <p>Disney's newest trackless ride is pure joy from start to finish. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>Animal Kingdom: Wild Encounters</h2>

    <h3>Kilimanjaro Safaris</h3>
    <p>A 20-minute open-air truck tour through an African savanna with real animals. Best time: early morning or late afternoon.</p>

    <h3>Na'vi River Journey</h3>
    <p>A gentle boat ride through the bioluminescent forests of Pandora. <strong>Height requirement:</strong> None</p>

    <h2>EPCOT: World Discovery</h2>

    <h3>Frozen Ever After</h3>
    <p>A boat ride through Arendelle featuring Anna, Elsa, and Olaf. Two small backward drops that are very mild.</p>

    <h2>Essential Tips for Parents</h2>

    <p><strong>Height Requirements to Know:</strong></p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>32 inches:</strong> Alien Swirling Saucers</li>
      <li><strong>40 inches:</strong> Slinky Dog Dash, Seven Dwarfs Mine Train</li>
    </ul>
